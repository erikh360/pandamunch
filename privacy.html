Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The Short Version

Panda Munch does not collect, store, or share any personal data. We don't know who you are and we don't want to.

What We Collect

Nothing. Panda Munch has no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no backend of any kind.

Game Center

If you choose to use Game Center (Apple's leaderboard and achievement service), your scores and Game Center identity are handled entirely by Apple under their own Privacy Policy. We never see or store this data. Game Center is optional — the game works fully without it.

Purchases

Your payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive no financial information, no email address, and no account details.

Children

Panda Munch does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. There is nothing to collect.

Changes

If this ever changes — for example if we add crash reporting in a future update — this policy will be updated before that version ships.
